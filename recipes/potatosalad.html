Potato Salad Recipe

Description

This creamy potato salad is a classic side dish that is perfect for picnics, barbecues, and family gatherings. It features tender potatoes, crunchy celery, and a tangy dressing made with mayonnaise, mustard, and vinegar. Serve it chilled for the best flavor.

Ingredients

  • 5 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions

  1. Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain and cool.
  2. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel, and chop.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, eggs, mayonnaise, onion, celery, mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  4. Sprinkle with paprika before serving.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
