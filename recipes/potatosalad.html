Potato Salad Recipe

Description

This classic potato salad recipe is a perfect side dish for picnics, barbecues, and family gatherings. It features tender potatoes, crunchy celery, and a creamy dressing made with mayonnaise and mustard. Serve it chilled for the best flavor.

Ingredients

  • 5 potatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped pickles
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and cool.
  2. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel, and chop.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, eggs, mayonnaise, mustard, celery, onion, pickles, salt, pepper, and parsley. Mix well to combine. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
