Chicken Breast Recipe

Description

This simple and delicious chicken breast recipe is perfect for a quick and healthy dinner. The chicken is seasoned with herbs and spices, then baked to perfection. Serve it with your favorite vegetables or a side salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the garlic powder, paprika, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the chicken breasts with olive oil, then sprinkle the seasoning mixture evenly over both sides of each chicken breast.
  4. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
